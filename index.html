Dancing UI is a personal playground built by a single developer to explore animations, interactions, and experimental user interface ideas. This site is inspired by modern AI tools like ChatGPT, OpenAI, DALL·E, GPT-4, and GPT-5 — not as official products, but as motivation to learn, build, and experiment. Everything here is handcrafted, tested, broken, fixed, and improved through curiosity and iteration. If you have feedback, ideas, or suggestions for new interactions, feel free to reach out at your@email.com. This project will keep evolving, one animation at a time.
